## 1. Per Square Foot Construction Costs in Hyderabad (2025-2026)

### Construction Cost by Quality Tier

| Quality Tier | Cost Range (per sq.ft.) | Typical Mid-Point |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | Rs. 1,400 - Rs. 1,800 | Rs. 1,600 |
| **Standard** | Rs. 1,800 - Rs. 2,300 | Rs. 2,050 |
| **Premium** | Rs. 2,300 - Rs. 3,000 | Rs. 2,700 |
| **Luxury / Ultra-Premium** | Rs. 3,000 - Rs. 5,000+ | Rs. 3,500+ |

### What's Included at Each Tier

**Basic Construction (Rs. 1,400 - 1,800/sq.ft.)**
- Standard cement (OPC 43 grade) and basic TMT steel
- Red clay bricks or fly ash bricks for walls
- Basic ceramic floor tiles (Rs. 30-50/sq.ft.)
- Standard sanitary fittings (Hindware/Parryware economy range)
- Basic electrical wiring with standard switches (Anchor/Roma)
- Single coat putty + acrylic emulsion paint (Tractor/Ace equivalent)
- Simple elevation with cement plaster finish
- Standard CPVC plumbing
- Basic aluminium windows
- Flush doors for interiors, single panel main door

**Standard Construction (Rs. 1,800 - 2,300/sq.ft.)**
- Premium cement (PPC/OPC 53 grade, UltraTech/ACC)
- Fe 500D TMT bars (TATA/JSW)
- AAC blocks or quality red bricks
- Vitrified floor tiles (Rs. 60-100/sq.ft., Kajaria/Somany)
- Branded sanitary fittings (Jaquar/Kohler mid-range)
- Concealed copper wiring with modular switches (Legrand/Schneider)
- Two-coat putty + premium emulsion (Asian Paints Royale/Dulux)
- Designed elevation with texture/stone cladding
- CPVC + some copper plumbing
- UPVC windows with mosquito mesh
- Teak frame doors with flush/panel shutters

**Premium Construction (Rs. 2,300 - 3,000/sq.ft.)**
- Premium cement brands with quality control testing
- High-grade Fe 500D TMT bars with mill test certificates
- AAC blocks with thermal insulation consideration
- Italian/Spanish vitrified or natural stone flooring
- Premium sanitary fittings (Kohler/Grohe/Duravit)
- Fully concealed wiring with premium modular switches (Legrand Arteor/Schneider ZENcelo)
- Premium paints with waterproofing (Royale Luxury/Dulux Velvet Touch)
- Designer elevation with composite materials, HPL cladding
- Full copper plumbing with branded fittings
- Premium UPVC or aluminium-clad windows with double glazing
- Solid teak wood doors with designer hardware

**Luxury / Ultra-Premium (Rs. 3,000 - 5,000+/sq.ft.)**
- Imported materials and custom specifications
- Advanced structural design with large spans
- Imported marble/granite/engineered wood flooring
- Ultra-premium international brand fittings (Hansgrohe/Villeroy & Boch)
- Smart home wiring with home automation systems
- Premium imported paints, special textures, Venetian plaster
- Bespoke elevation with glass facades, SS elements
- Smart plumbing with sensor-based fixtures
- Structural glazing, floor-to-ceiling windows
- Imported doors, biometric locks, designer handles

## 4. Professional Fees

### Fee Structure for Residential Projects in Hyderabad

| Professional | Fee Structure | Typical Cost for Rs. 1 Cr Project |
|---|---|---|
| **Architect** | 5-10% of construction cost (or Rs. 30-80/sq.ft.) | Rs. 5-10 Lakhs |
| **Structural Engineer** | 1.5-3% of construction cost (or Rs. 10-25/sq.ft.) | Rs. 1.5-3 Lakhs |
| **Interior Designer** | 8-15% of interior work cost (or Rs. 50-150/sq.ft. design fee) | Rs. 3-8 Lakhs |
| **PMC (Project Management Consultant)** | 5-8% of total project cost | Rs. 5-8 Lakhs |
| **Vastu Consultant** | Fixed fee + per sq.ft. basis | Rs. 15,000 - 1,00,000 |

### Detailed Breakdown

**Architect Fees:**
- New/emerging architects: 4-6% of construction cost
- Established architects: 8-10% of construction cost
- Celebrity/high-profile architects: 12-15%+ of construction cost
- Per sq.ft. design fee: Rs. 30 - 80 per sq.ft.
- Site supervision fee (additional): 1-2% of construction cost
- Council of Architecture recommended: 6-15% based on project size

**Structural Engineer:**
- Design fee: Rs. 10-25 per sq.ft.
- Soil testing + foundation design: Rs. 25,000 - 75,000
- Complete structural design (G+2): Rs. 50,000 - 2,00,000

**Interior Designer:**
- Design consultation only: Rs. 10-100 per sq.ft.
- Design + execution: 8-15% of total interior work value
- Flat fee for complete home: Rs. 2,00,000 - 10,00,000+

**PMC (Project Management Consultant):**
- Full project management: 5-8% of total construction cost
- Partial supervision: 3-5%
- Per visit fee model: Rs. 2,000-5,000 per visit

**Vastu Consultant:**
- Online consultation: Rs. 4,500 - 15,000
- On-site visit (single): Rs. 7,500 - 25,000
- Detailed property analysis: Rs. 11,000 - 50,000
- Per sq.ft. basis: Rs. 15-25 per sq.ft.
- Construction-stage consultations (multiple visits): Rs. 50,000 - 1,00,000

## 7. Timeline Expectations

### Overall Construction Timelines

| House Size | Construction Type | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **1,000 - 2,000 sq.ft. (G+0 or G+1)** | Standard | 10 - 15 months |
| **2,000 - 4,000 sq.ft. (G+1 or G+2)** | Standard | 14 - 20 months |
| **4,000+ sq.ft. Villa/Mansion** | Premium/Luxury | 18 - 30 months |

### Phase-wise Timeline Breakdown

| Phase | Duration | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Pre-Construction** | 2-4 months | Architect plans, structural design, GHMC approvals, soil testing |
| **2. Foundation & Substructure** | 1.5 - 3 months | Excavation, PCC, footing, column stubs, plinth beam, backfill |
| **3. Superstructure (per floor)** | 2 - 4 months per floor | Columns, beams, slab casting, curing |
| **4. Masonry (Walls)** | 1.5 - 3 months | External walls, internal partitions, lintel beams |
| **5. Plastering & Waterproofing** | 1 - 2 months | Internal/external plaster, terrace waterproofing |
| **6. MEP (Mechanical/Electrical/Plumbing)** | 1.5 - 3 months | Wiring, switches, plumbing fixtures, fittings |
| **7. Finishing Stage 1** | 2 - 4 months | Flooring, door/window installation, basic carpentry |
| **8. Finishing Stage 2** | 2 - 4 months | Painting, polishing, hardware, final fixtures, cleaning |
| **9. Interiors (if included)** | 2 - 4 months | Modular kitchen, wardrobes, false ceiling, furnishing |

### Detailed Timeline for a Typical 2,500 sq.ft. G+1 House

| Month | Phase | Activities |
|---|---|---|
| Month 1-3 | Pre-construction | Design finalization, approvals, contractor selection |
| Month 4 | Foundation | Site clearing, excavation, PCC, footing |
| Month 5 | Substructure | Column stubs, plinth beam, backfilling, DPC |
| Month 6-7 | Ground floor structure | Columns, beams, slab casting, curing |
| Month 8-9 | First floor structure | Repeat for first floor + roof slab |
| Month 10-11 | Masonry | Brickwork, plastering (internal + external) |
| Month 12 | MEP rough-in | Electrical conduits, plumbing lines, waterproofing |
| Month 13-14 | Flooring & doors | Tile/stone installation, door/window fitting |
| Month 15-16 | Finishing | Painting, final electrical, plumbing fixtures |
| Month 17-18 | Interiors & handover | Kitchen, wardrobes, final touch-ups, cleaning |

### Seasonal Considerations for Hyderabad

| Season | Months | Impact on Construction |
|---|---|---|
| **Best Season** | October - March | Dry weather, ideal for foundation and concrete work |
| **Summer** | April - May | Hot weather, needs extra curing, worker productivity drops |
| **Monsoon** | June - September | **2-4 month delay possible.** Waterlogging, slow curing, site inaccessibility |

**Pro Tip:** Start foundation work in October-November to avoid monsoon delays. Structure should be complete before the next monsoon season.

## 8. Hidden Costs Most People Miss

### The 10-25% You Didn't Budget For

Most contractors quote only the basic civil construction cost. The following hidden costs can add **10-25% to your total budget** and are often discovered mid-project.

| Hidden Cost Item | Estimated Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Soil Testing** | Rs. 5,000 - 15,000 | Mandatory for proper foundation design. Skipping this can lead to cracks worth Rs. 3-5 Lakhs later |
| **Plot Levelling/Preparation** | Rs. 30,000 - 2,00,000 | If plot is uneven or low-lying |
| **Compound/Boundary Wall** | Rs. 1,50,000 - 5,00,000 | Precast: Rs. 70-110/sq.ft. Brick: Rs. 150-300/sq.ft. For 200 sq.yd. plot |
| **Landscaping** | Rs. 1,00,000 - 10,00,000+ | Basic: Rs. 50-200/sq.ft. Premium: Rs. 200-500+/sq.ft. |
| **Rainwater Harvesting** | Rs. 20,000 - 80,000 | Mandatory in Hyderabad. During construction: 25-30% cheaper than retrofitting |
| **Solar Panel Setup** | Rs. 1,20,000 - 5,00,000 | 3 kW system: ~Rs. 1.22 L (with PM Surya Ghar subsidy). Without subsidy: ~Rs. 2 L. 5 kW: Rs. 2.5-3.5 L |
| **Backup Power (Inverter)** | Rs. 15,000 - 50,000 | Basic inverter + battery for essential loads |
| **Backup Power (Generator)** | Rs. 30,000 - 2,00,000 | 5 kW generator: ~Rs. 30,000. Whole-house: Rs. 1-2 L |
| **Parking Area** | Rs. 50,000 - 3,00,000 | Tiled/concrete parking for 1-2 cars. Covered car porch adds more |
| **Security Systems** | Rs. 20,000 - 2,00,000 | CCTV (4-8 cameras): Rs. 15,000-40,000. Full automated system: Rs. 20,000-35,000. Smart home: Rs. 50,000-2,00,000 |
| **Elevation/Facade** | Rs. 2,00,000 - 15,00,000+ | HPL cladding, stone, texture. Often excluded from base quotation |
| **Borewell** | Rs. 50,000 - 2,00,000 | Depends on depth (200-400 ft. common in Hyderabad) |
| **Sump + Overhead Tank** | Rs. 50,000 - 1,50,000 | Underground sump (5000-10000 litres) + overhead tank |
| **Municipal Connections** | Rs. 25,000 - 75,000 | Water, sewage, electricity connections |
| **Material Wastage & Logistics** | 1-2% of total cost | Handling, lifting, unloading, re-transporting |
| **Design Changes Mid-Project** | 5-15% cost overrun | Every change order costs money and time |
| **Temporary Construction** | Rs. 20,000 - 75,000 | Site office, material storage shed, watchman shelter |
