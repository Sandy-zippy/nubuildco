## 1. Higgsfield AI Platform Overview

### What Is Higgsfield?

Higgsfield AI is a generative AI video platform (web-based, mobile-first) that transforms text prompts and source images into high-fidelity, cinematic video clips. Founded in 2023, headquartered in San Francisco, it has raised $138M+ in funding and is considered a leading AI video tool for creators and marketers.

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Image-to-Video** | Upload a single image and animate it with cinematic camera motion. This is the primary workflow for our ad. |
| **Cinema Studio** | 80+ cinematic camera presets: crash zooms, dolly-ins, pans, drone shots, overheads, boltcam angles. |
| **Click to Ad** | Paste a product/landing page URL and auto-generate a 9:16 video ad with AI avatar, captions, and script. |
| **Lipsync** | Create talking-head / UGC-style videos with AI avatar lip-sync. Ideal for explainer content. |
| **Sora 2 Trends** | Trend-driven ad templates powered by OpenAI Sora 2, with style presets like "Luxury Ad" and "TikTok Edit." |
| **Text Overlays** | 25+ caption presets with hard-coded typography, or "no caption" for clean output. |
| **Audio** | Audio presets or custom soundscape descriptions (voice + music + SFX). Auto-synchronizes beat to visuals. |
| **Multi-Model** | Access to 15+ AI models (Sora 2, Kling 2.6, Google Veo 3.1, WAN 2.5) under one subscription. |

## 4. Higgsfield-Specific Production Instructions

### Prerequisites

1. **Account:** Create an account at https://higgsfield.ai
2. **Plan:** Subscribe to the Pro plan ($29/mo) minimum for commercial rights and faster rendering.
3. **Images:** Have all 9 project images ready on your device (see Section 7 for file names).
4. **External editor:** Have CapCut (free), Adobe Premiere Pro, or DaVinci Resolve ready for stitching clips together and adding text overlays, voiceover, and music.

### Strategy: Why We Use Image-to-Video (Not Click-to-Ad)

Higgsfield's **Click to Ad** feature auto-generates a single 12-second ad from a URL. However, for a premium brand like NUBUILDCo, we want **full creative control** over:
- Which specific images appear and in what order
- Camera motion style per shot
- Pacing and transitions
- Text overlay design

Therefore, the recommended workflow is:
1. Generate individual 3-5 second cinematic clips from each image using **Image-to-Video** in **Cinema Studio**
2. Stitch the clips together in an external editor
3. Add text overlays, voiceover, and music in the editor

#### Step 4: Select Quality and Model Settings

| Setting | Recommended Value |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Model** | "Standard" (Premium) or "Turbo" for faster renders |
| **Duration** | 5 seconds (maximum available per clip) |
| **Enhance** | ON (toggle the Enhance switch) |
| **Steps** | 50 (higher = better quality, slower render) |
| **Seed** | Leave as random for first attempt; note the seed number if you get a result you like, so you can reproduce it |
| **Aspect Ratio** | 9:16 (vertical) for social media ads |

#### Step 5: Generate and Review
- Click **Generate**
- Wait for the render (5-30 minutes depending on model and queue)
- Review the output. If the motion is too aggressive or artifacts appear:
  - Simplify the prompt (remove complex motion descriptions)
  - Try a different camera preset
  - Reduce the "Steps" to 30 for faster iteration, then re-render at 50 once satisfied
- Download the MP4 when satisfied

#### Step 6: Generate Multiple Takes
- For each shot, generate 2-3 variations by slightly modifying the prompt or changing the camera preset
- Pick the best take for each shot

### Post-Production Assembly (External Editor)

Since Higgsfield generates individual 3-5 second clips, you must assemble the final ad in an external editor.

#### Recommended Tool: CapCut (Free) or Adobe Premiere Pro

**Assembly Steps:**

1. **Import all clips** into your editor timeline in script order
2. **Trim each clip** to the exact duration specified in the script (see second-by-second breakdown)
3. **Add transitions:**
   - First shot: Hard cut from black (fade-in 0.3s)
   - Between shots: Cross-dissolve, 0.3-0.5 seconds
   - Final shot: Fade to black, 0.5 seconds
4. **Add text overlays** matching the script:
   - Font: A premium serif or clean sans-serif (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or Inter)
   - Colors: White text with subtle drop shadow on interior shots; gold/cream for CTA
   - Position: Centered or lower-third as specified in the script
   - Animation: Simple fade-in or scale-up (0.2s). Nothing flashy.
5. **Add voiceover:**
   - Record using a professional male voice (Indian English accent preferred for local trust)
   - Or use ElevenLabs / PlayHT AI voice with a "confident, mature, warm" male preset
   - Align voiceover timing to the second-by-second script
6. **Add music:** (See Section 6 for recommendations)
   - Place under the voiceover at approximately 20% volume
   - Swell the music during the visual-only moment (0:07-0:09 in 15s version)
   - Fade music out under the final CTA
7. **Add NUBUILDCo logo** on the final frame:
   - Logo files available at: `/Users/sandy/Downloads/Claude Code/Branding X Newbuild/Logo Files/`
   - Use the white/cream version on a dark or blurred background
8. **Export:**
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16)
   - Codec: H.264
   - Bitrate: 8-12 Mbps
   - Frame rate: 30fps

## 6. Music and Audio Recommendations

### Mood Profile

The music must convey: **premium craftsmanship, quiet confidence, architectural beauty, aspirational living.** It should NOT feel: salesy, urgent, generic corporate, or Bollywood.

### Genre Description for Higgsfield Custom Audio

If using Higgsfield's built-in audio feature, enter this description:

```
Soft ambient piano with warm electronic textures. Minimal, spacious, architectural.
Think luxury real estate commercial. Subtle bass warmth. No percussion for the first 10 seconds,
then gentle rhythmic pulse builds. Elegant and restrained. Premium brand tone.
```

### Recommended Royalty-Free Tracks (External Sources)

Search for these on Artlist, Epidemic Sound, or YouTube Audio Library:

| Source | Search Terms | Why It Works |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Artlist.io** | "Minimal piano ambient architecture" | Clean, cinematic, no vocals |
| **Epidemic Sound** | "Luxury real estate" or "Premium lifestyle ambient" | Pre-tagged for commercial/brand use |
| **YouTube Audio Library** | "Ambient" + "Cinematic" filter | Free, no attribution needed |
| **Uppbeat.io** | "Elegant" + "Architecture" | Free tier available with attribution |

### Specific Track Style References

These are NOT tracks to use (they are copyrighted), but they describe the exact mood to search for:

- **Hans Zimmer's "Time" (Inception)** — The opening 30 seconds: sparse piano, building warmth, emotional gravity. Search for "cinematic piano build" to find similar royalty-free options.
- **Ólafur Arnalds style** — Minimal piano + subtle electronic ambient layers. Search for "neoclassical ambient" or "post-minimal piano."
- **Architectural Digest video style** — Understated, textural ambient. Search for "editorial ambient" or "design documentary score."

### Voiceover Specifications

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Gender** | Male (preferred for authority/trust with HNI audience) |
| **Accent** | Indian English — educated, clear, warm. Not Americanized. |
| **Tone** | Confident, calm, knowledgeable. Like a trusted advisor, not a salesperson. |
| **Pace** | Measured and deliberate. No rushing. Pauses between key phrases. |
| **AI Voice Option** | ElevenLabs "Raj" or "Arjun" preset; or PlayHT "Professional Indian Male" |
| **Recording Note** | If recording live, use a condenser mic in a treated room. No echo. No background noise. |
