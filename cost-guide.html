The Real Cost — Breaking Down Every Rupee
Per square foot rates across four quality tiers, and what's actually included at each level.

What's Included: Basic (₹1,400-1,800/sq.ft.)

Standard cement (OPC 43), red clay bricks, basic ceramic tiles (₹30-50/sq.ft.), standard sanitary fittings (Hindware economy), basic wiring with standard switches, single-coat paint, simple cement plaster elevation, CPVC plumbing, basic aluminium windows, flush doors.

What's Included: Standard (₹1,800-2,300/sq.ft.)

Premium cement (UltraTech/ACC), AAC blocks, vitrified tiles (Kajaria/Somany ₹60-100/sq.ft.), Jaquar mid-range fittings, concealed copper wiring with modular switches, two-coat putty + Asian Paints Royale, designed elevation with texture, UPVC windows with mesh, teak frame doors.

What's Included: Premium (₹2,300-3,000/sq.ft.)

Quality-tested premium cement, high-grade TMT with mill certificates, Italian/Spanish vitrified or natural stone, Kohler/Grohe fittings, premium modular switches (Legrand Arteor), waterproof premium paints, designer elevation with HPL cladding, full copper plumbing, double-glazed UPVC windows, solid teak doors.

What's Included: Luxury (₹3,000-5,000+/sq.ft.)

Imported materials, imported marble/engineered wood, Hansgrohe/Villeroy & Boch fittings, smart home automation wiring, Venetian plaster finishes, glass facade with SS elements, structural glazing, imported doors with biometric locks.

Where Your Money Goes

Pro Tip

Don't just compare per sq.ft. rates — always ask what's included. A ₹1,800 quote that excludes elevation, compound wall, and electrical will end up costing more than a ₹2,200 quote that includes everything.
